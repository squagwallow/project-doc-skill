# Discovery Summary — Week 1 Kickoff

Kickoff call with Priya (founder). 60 minutes. Present: operator, Priya. Jo and Marcus not present — will be interviewed separately in week 1.

## Observed shape

Lumen runs on Notion as a single coordination hub. Client database holds one page per retained client (10 active). Project database holds one page per active engagement. CRM database syncs from HubSpot Free via an existing Zapier pipe that Priya set up 18 months ago. Metricool is fully separate — each client has a brand space, and Jo works inside Metricool directly for posting and analytics. Xero is Priya's domain entirely; billable hours are tracked in a Notion database and manually transcribed to Xero invoices once a month.

## Priya's stated pain

Three things came up unprompted: "I'm copying Metricool numbers into Notion every Monday morning and it's eating 3 hours"; "every new lead pings me on my phone for qualification, even at 10pm"; "I don't actually know how much time we spent on Client X last month without sitting down with the billable hours DB for half an hour." In priority order she would fix social reporting first, then lead qualification, then invoicing reconciliation.

## Team dynamics (observed)

Priya is bottlenecking because she hired Jo and Marcus for execution, not ownership. Both are capable. Jo has been at Lumen 14 months and knows Metricool better than Priya does. Marcus has 8 months and has built client rapport but has never been given independent decision authority. Priya flagged guilt about over-controlling but also flagged legitimate concern about brand-voice consistency on social.

## Open questions going into week 2

- How strict are the deliverable-review requirements Priya actually needs? (Likely loosenable.)
- What is Marcus's read on lead qualification criteria? (Interview scheduled.)
- Does Jo want full Metricool → Notion reporting ownership or just the reporting automation? (Distinction matters.)
